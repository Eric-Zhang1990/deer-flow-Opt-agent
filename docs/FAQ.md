## Where's the name Egent Trader come from?

Egent Trader is short for **D**eep **E**xploration and **E**fficient **R**esearch **Flow**. It is named after the deer, which is a symbol of gentleness and elegance. We hope Egent Trader can bring a gentle and elegant deep research experience to you.
