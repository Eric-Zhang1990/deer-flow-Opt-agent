## Where's the name Opt Agent come from?

Opt Agent is short for **D**eep **E**xploration and **E**fficient **R**esearch **Flow**. It is named after the deer, which is a symbol of gentleness and elegance. We hope Opt Agent can bring a gentle and elegant deep research experience to you.
